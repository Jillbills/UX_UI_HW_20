Edúcate Ya Case Study

What is Edúcate Ya?

Portland, OR based Non-Profit Organization

We began by analyzing their website to learn more about the non-profit organization and to identify areas that were most in need of a redesign.



Project Overview

The Problem

Edúcate Ya hasn’t been reaching it’s targeted audience. Their homepage averaged an 85% bounce rate in Oct to Nov 2021, which is nearly twice as high as a typical website bounce rate.

The Solution

By redesigning Edúcate Ya’s website to be more visually engaging, we can help improve usability, navigation, and user engagement.

Definition

Problem Statement

By redesigning Edúcate Ya’s website to be more visually engaging, we can help increase engagement with and participation in the organization’s classes and events.

We also believe that a website that is easier to maintain for the organization and more delightful for users to use will result in an increase in donations to Edúcate Ya.

UX Hypothesis

The primary user of Edúcate Ya’s website is not typically the direct user of their education and leadership programs.

Instead, the primary user might be someone who has better Internet access and proficiency navigating online resources that tells the direct user by word of mouth or refers them to the website.

proved to be true!

Conclusion

Reflections

  • Learning more about the actual users (18 to 24 year old demographic) from the Google Analytics report helped us redesign the style of the Edúcate Ya website to appeal to the appropriate targeted audience.

  • It was very rewarding to see that the stakeholders began implementing some of our redesign recommendations soon after our team presented it to them!

Future Developments

  • Increase copy font size for mobile to at least 16px for better readability.

  • Implementing a “Back to Top” button, which is currently designed but not active yet.
