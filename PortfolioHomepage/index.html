About

Hi! I'm Jill Van,
UX Designerwith an extensive healthcare background in Occupational Therapy, specializing in cardiology. Certificate in UX/UI Design from University of Oregon’s Bootcamp. Bachelor’s of Science in Occupational Therapy from Eastern Washington University.

Dedicated, self-directed, diligent worker. Works well independently or collaborating in a team. Organized yet flexible, creative thinker. Other designers recognize my forward-thinking and strong skills in communicating a vision to create positive change.

I also co-own, operate a small business on Etsy, designing and creating vinyl decals. Additional roles: customer service, bookkeeping. Love of art and design motivated my career shift from healthcare to UX Design. My passion for enhancing user experiences and proactive problem solving make me a valuable addition to any team.
